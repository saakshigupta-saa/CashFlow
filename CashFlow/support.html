Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an expense?

Enter your salary, expense details, choose a category, and click Add Expense.

How do I delete an expense?

Click the trash icon beside any transaction.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored securely in your browser using Local Storage.

Can I export my report?

Yes. Click the Download PDF button from the dashboard.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your information never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.
